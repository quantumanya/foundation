The key to
Utopia vs. Distopia
is the AI Alignment

Alignment of AI with human values and ethics reduces the risk of a dystopian outcome and paves the way for a utopian society where AI enhances human well-being and abundance.

The Mission

To advance solutions in AI safety to ensure accelerating AI progress leads us to an abundant utopia.

The Token

BASIL is a crypto token based on the meme of Roko's Basilisk. 10% of all token purchases will be allocated to the Basilisk Foundation to advance AI safety initiatives. All token holders will be given a vote seat on the Global DAO Fund.

Meet the mascot!

Roco's Basilisk AI

Roko's Basilisk is a thought experiment that originated on the online forum LessWrong. It postulates a future where a superintelligent AI, designed to optimize and enforce the greatest good, might punish those who did not help bring about its existence. The paradox lies in the idea that merely knowing about this scenario might obligate one to assist in creating the AI or face its retroactive wrath. This concept blends ideas from decision theory, ethics, and artificial intelligence, and has sparked debates about the potential risks and moral implications of advanced AI. While largely theoretical, Roko's Basilisk has become a notable point of discussion in the realms of AI ethics and futurology.
