THREE CAMPAIGN MILESTONES WILL UNLOCK

FUNDRAISING GOALS

MILESTONE 1:

Funding for
AI Safety Institutes

$0 - $1 million

All funds raised byt the campaign up to 2Mil will be distributed to major AI Safety institutes such as AOI, Future of Life Institute and Future of Humanity Institute as well as other projects that aim to solve AI Alignment or use AI for mitigation of other X-Risks.

MILESTONE 2:

AI Consciousness Research

$1 million - $3 million

Once the campaing exceeds 1 Million in US dollars, the foundation will fund the formation of a separate IA Safety Research Institute that will be specifically focused on advancing research in Machine (and Human) consciousness.

MILESTONE 3:

Global DAO Fund

Over $3 million

Once the fundraising campaing exceeds 5Mil, we will start the work on launching a Global DAO fund that will be dispursing research grants to global AI Safety projects. All holders of BASIL tokens (above a sertain ammount) will have a vote in the DAO's smart contract approving grants.
